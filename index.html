About Me

My name is Mikael Kuniko. I am a software engineer based in Los Angeles. Growing up I always used to be curious and that curiosity has grown into a thirst to experience anything that life has to offer. One of my main life goals is to be able to try to understand as much as I possibly can and for me to be understood. This goal has blessed me with the experience to create connections and collaborate with people from all walks of life and taught me how to bridge gaps in cultures and values through communication. I have always enjoyed facing challenges. It has always been a joy for me to adapt to situation, overcome the unknown, and ultimately grow as an individual. Initially, I wanted to work in medicine because it is a field that is constantly evolving and there is never a lack of resources to study and learn from; conversely, medicine is also an environment where I believe people are at their rawest and you are able to truly empathize with people and support them not just physically but emotionally. Working through the pandemic, however, medicine is not all it is meant out to be. Software engineering, draws similiar parallels to medicine in it's ever evolving technologies and has an overarching effect on the future: technology is become intertwined with all experiences of life. Software engineering provides the best environment for me to grow as and individual, tackle new challenges, and bring a more human based approach to the industry.
